The Risk

When a warehouse worker's Android session fails after an update, they cannot see their W-2/1099 classification, cannot verify their payment routing, and cannot complete their shift check-in. That is not a UX problem ‚Äî it is a wage and hour compliance event. Enterprise clients choosing between GigSmart and Instawork will measure fill rate reliability. Android session failures directly reduce fill rates.
