Pain pattern

The public feedback points to workflow drag, not product-market doubt.

Healico solves a real problem. The stronger opportunity is to remove the awkward steps around handoff, multi-wound clarity, and session continuity so clinicians stay confident when the caseload gets messy.

Pain 01

Export friction

Users publicly asked where PDF export lives. The latest release notes position full-history PDF export as a key feature, but still only available on request.

Pain 02

Wound ambiguity

Clinicians want more precise location handling and clearer distinction when several wounds sit on the same body area.

Pain 03

Reconnect risk

Repeated disconnect and access-recovery complaints suggest trust can drop right when care documentation needs to feel reliable.

Prototype

One tighter mobile motion: capture, label, share.

This concept narrows the scope to the most credible win: turning a completed wound visit into a clean, labeled, shareable artifact without support dependency or clinician guesswork.

Current pattern
Step 1: complete visit notes
Step 2: search for export or leave the app without a clean artifact
Step 3: manually explain which wound is which if several live on the same zone
Step 4: lose orientation if auth times out mid-flow
Improved pattern
Step 1: finish visit and auto-group wound entries
Step 2: tap a visible primary CTA: `Share visit pack`
Step 3: send PDF, secure summary, or team handoff from one surface
Step 4: if interrupted, reopen exactly where the draft left off
