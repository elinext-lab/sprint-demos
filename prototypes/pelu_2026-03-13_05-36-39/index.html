What Public Signals Say

The current public mobile story is still in transition.

PELU's official mobile page markets AI coaching, medication support, appointments, community, biometric security, and offline access. The live product proof is thinner than the promise: the real public app entry point is the web app, the visible store badges do not land on native listings, and the page notes that app-store reviews are still coming later.

Prototype Angle

Instead of redesigning everything, this concept fixes the narrowest, highest-leverage layer: the jump from public interest to a useful first action.

  • Clarify what is live now: web app, native install, or waitlist.
  • Explain the care path by country and support type before users hit login friction.
  • Start the day-one adherence loop immediately with a trusted plan card.
  • Give Oliver a mobile experience that matches his orchestration language.
