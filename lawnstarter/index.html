GPS Permission Loop ‚Äî 3‚Äì4 re-enables per job
CRITICAL

After a recent app update, providers are prompted to enable GPS 3‚Äì4 times per job. On Samsung, Huawei, and some Pixel devices, the OS battery optimizer kills the GPS session between prompts, creating an infinite re-request loop. GPS confirmation is required to trigger job-start and job-complete payment events.

Gold-tier provider review
"A recent app update prompted me to enable GPS 3‚Äì4 times just to mark a job complete. I only allow Google Maps to enable GPS... I've given LawnStarter until July 1 to fix this before I cancel my service."

Root cause: Android 10‚Äì13 background location permission redesign + OEM battery optimizer (Samsung One UI, Huawei EMUI). The app re-requests permission per GPS event instead of maintaining a persistent Foreground Service.

Photo Upload Failure ‚Äî required step for job completion and payment
HIGH

Before/after photo upload is a required step in LawnStarter's job completion flow. The Android app does not accept photos or crashes during upload on Android 14 devices. Failed upload = unclosed job = no payment triggered.

User review (verbatim)
"The app won't accept uploading pictures... the app loves to restart itself when trying to upload photos."
Payment blocked Android 14 break Job cannot close

Root cause: Android 14 changed media permissions (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES). Apps not updated to new permission model break on Android 14 devices. OOM crash during upload = app not handling large camera images with proper downsampling.
