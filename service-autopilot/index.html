Critical Bug #2: Progressive Cache Degradation (Reinstall Required)
‚ñº Demo

What happens: App accumulates data over 4-6 months until it won't open. The only fix is full uninstall + reinstall. Happens cyclically ‚Äî every quarter for heavy users.

"After a few months, the app gets so slow it won't open. You have to delete and reinstall. This has happened 3 times now." ‚Äî Google Play

Root cause: No SQLite/CoreData pruning; delta sync not implemented; all records re-fetched every session = cache grows indefinitely.

Storage accumulation over time
Month 1: 45 MB ‚Äî Normal
Month 3: 280 MB ‚Äî Slowing
Month 6: 890 MB ‚Äî Unusable

With delta sync + LRU cache pruning (2 sprint cycles), storage stays flat after initial load regardless of usage duration.
