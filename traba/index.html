Fix scope: Targeted fix, no architectural changes required. Expand geofence radius with GPS confidence interval, configure Android foreground service to survive Doze mode, add WiFi/network hybrid fallback when GPS HDOP drops below threshold.
Root cause: Steel and concrete walls in warehouses attenuate GPS satellite signals by 15‚Äì30 dB. The device reports a location 60‚Äì100m off ground truth. Your geofence triggers a false exit. Compounding this: Android's Doze mode (screen-off battery optimization) can suspend the FOREGROUND_SERVICE background location ‚Äî if the service isn't configured with a persistent notification and the correct wake-lock, GPS stops updating mid-shift. The fix: wider geofence radius, network-location hybrid fallback, and proper Doze-mode exemption.
