The Invisible Problem

When I Work shows 4.8 stars on both iOS and Android. The rating looks fine. The damage is invisible: clock-out requests that silently fail, GPS geofences that block clock-in in every indoor environment, and push notifications that never arrive. These failures do not generate 1-star reviews — they generate RevOps tickets, timesheet corrections, and manager churn. Your own Help Center documents these failure modes. The rating hides the cost.
