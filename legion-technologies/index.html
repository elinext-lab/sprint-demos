Android users install because HR requires it. They don't engage deeply. They don't review. Platform mechanics — not UX — are the cause.

Why enterprise WFM Android engagement is the business metric that matters

Legion's enterprise clients include the largest Android-dominant workforces in the US. Dollar General's average store associate earns ~$12/hour. The sub-$300 Android market (Motorola E-series, Samsung A-series) owns 70-80% of this demographic. These workers are not using iPhones.

When Android delivery gaps cause session lockouts, missed schedule notifications, and UI freezes on budget hardware — workers stop using the app proactively. They call managers. Manual processes return. The ROI case that won the enterprise deal weakens at renewal time.
